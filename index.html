Our Four Grain Bourbon story starts with two Colorado family farms where high quality corn and heirloom varietals of wheat, barley and rye are grown. Those grains find their expression during our labor-intensive cooking technique, bringing each grain's individual character to life, while open-air sour mash adds soulful complexity. After four years in 53-gallon charred oak barrels, influenced by our high-altitude climate, an entirely novel bourbon unfolds.

AROMAS:

Orange blossoms compliment notes of black tea, honey and dusty pepper.

PALATE:

Pekoe tea, orange peel, cinnamon and vanilla custard. Hints of sweet tobacco and spice lead to a rich, dry finish.

THE DETAILS:

Mash Bill: 60% Colorado corn, 20% heirloom wheat, 10% heirloom rye, 10% heirloom malted barley
Aged 4+ years in new 53-gallon charred oak barrels
95 Proof · 47.5% ABV
